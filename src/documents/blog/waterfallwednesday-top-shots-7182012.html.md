If you want to get in on the action, share your best waterfall shot next Wendnesday on Google+ and include the hashtag in the body of the post. If waterfalls aren't your thing, check out the <a href="http://ericleslie.com/guides/daily-photography-themes-googleplus">full list of Daily Photography Themes</a> to discover something else.
