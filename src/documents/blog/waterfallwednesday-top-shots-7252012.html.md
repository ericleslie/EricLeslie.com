I get so excited when I see #WaterfallWednesday trending on Google+. Sometimes it will trend in the morning and fade off into the afternoon, but yesterday I saw it in the top five all day long. My hat is off to all of you that contributed so many great shots.

If you missed out and want to get in on the action, share your best waterfall shot next Wendnesday on Google+ and include the hashtag in the body of the post. If waterfalls aren’t your thing, check out the full list of <a href="http://ericleslie.com/guides/daily-photography-themes-googleplus">Daily Photography Themes</a> to discover something else.
